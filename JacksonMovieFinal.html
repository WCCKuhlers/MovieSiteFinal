Introduction

Released on September 8, 2017, the movie IT, is a horror film based on the original film released in the year 1990.

The movie starts off by introducing one of the main characters named "Billy" constructing a paper boat for his little brother "Georgie". And after heading outside and playing with his paper boat in the streets of Dairy, Maine, he ends up meeting a clown in the sewers named "Pennywise".

    Actors Featured

  • Finn "Richie" Wolfhard
  • Sophia "Beverly" Lillis
  • Bill "Pennywise" Skarsgard
  • Jaeden "Bill" Lieberher
  • Chosen "Mike" Jacobs

    My Favorite Scenes

  1. Pennywise Fight
  2. The Build-up
  3. Meeting Pennywise

Reason for my favorite scene

The primary reason for my favorite scene in the movie "IT" being the final fight with Pennywise at the end of the movie is that when Pennywise is defeated, he falls into the nearby sewer drain and it starts to build up suspense for the next chapter of the movie.

Reason for my 2nd favorite scene

The reason why my 2nd favorite scene in the movie is the build-up is because the build-up is meant to create a emotional feeling in the audience that makes the audience think "What is going to happen next?"
