What is Baby Driver

Baby Driver is a Thriller, Action, and Crime movie about a young man named Baby who is roped into working for a crime boss as a getaway driver but meets the love of his life, Deborah, and wants to get out of the business. He is hearing impaired with a condition called tinnitus and uses music to drown out a constant ringing in his ear. He also uses the music to live his life and commit his robberies all while going along with the beat of the music also using it to convey his mood.
