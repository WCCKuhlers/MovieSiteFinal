Summary

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-verse is a computer animated film made by Sony Pictures Animation
and Columbia Pictures. This is the first featured film on the character Miles Morales (voiced by
Shameik More). The Character Miles Morales is in world where the original spiderman ,Peter Parker,
is killed by a notorious villain named King Pin. Before Peter parker dies, he fights King Pin and
crashes into King Pin's time machine. This causes a dimension to split in many alternative worlds
with different versions of spiderman. These versions of spiderman are now in Miles world. Since
the original spiderman is dead in Miles Morales world, he is next in line to be spiderman. Now
Miles Morales has to defeat King Pin and return the other versions of spiderman to their original
worlds.
